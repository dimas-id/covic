This review summarises the developments in adhesion, VOC emissions, coatings, transparent conductive coatings, hybrid organic-inorganic coatings, UV curing, biocidal coatings, paints, weathering, wood coatings, surface treatment of concrete, metal corrosion, pigments, film formation, printing, modelling fluid flow in printing, dot gain and inkjet printing reported inSurface Coatings International Part B: Coatings Transactions,88, 2005.
