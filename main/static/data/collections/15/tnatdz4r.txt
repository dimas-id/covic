The redistribution of healthcare resources for coronavirus 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic has resulted in unintentional neglect of essential non-COVID-19 care (1). In low- and lower-middle income countries, the already overstretched healthcare systems have crumbled under the COVID-19 pressure (1). In India, the first COVID-19 case was detected on January 30, and a nation-wide total lockdown was announced from March 25 until May 3, 2020 followed by a partial lockdown.
