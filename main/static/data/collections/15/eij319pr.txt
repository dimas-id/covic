Complex fistula treatment still possess a lot of difficulties and there is no gold standard treatment so far. Only a few small studies using PermacolTM collagen paste (Covidien plc, Gosport, Hampshire, UK) have been reported to date [1-5]. The success rate varies between 47.6% and 77.5%. Here we report a case of a complex transsphincteric fistula successfully treated with PermacolTM collagen paste. This article is protected by copyright. All rights reserved.
