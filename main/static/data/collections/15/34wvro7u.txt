• The condition of some patients with COVID-19 progress rapidly and require aggressive treatments and intensive care, and most of them required endotracheal intubation for mechanical ventilation. • Emergency endotracheal intubation in critically ill patients with COVID-19 has posed a huge challenge to the expertise and self-protection of anesthesiologists. • Anesthesiologists from the frontline of Wuhan, China, of fighting against the epidemic brought first-hand experience about emergency endotracheal intubation in critically ill patients with COVID-19 to colleagues.
