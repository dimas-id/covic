• Surgery for Mucosal Head and neck Cancers needs special consideration as they are AGP, placing the OT staff at a major risk. • Nevertheless all such procedures are considered semi emergencies and they cannot be postponed indefinitely. • Support Machinery of the Government and the co-operation of the people are the key factors favouring best practice of head and neck cancer surgery amidst the current COVID 19 crisis. • Waiting for atleast a period of 2 weeks would a prudent solution to rule out coexistence of COVID 19 infection in low risk cases without any symptoms.
