Whilst urgent gynecological and oncologic operations have continued, the SARS-CoV-2 (COVID-19) pandemic has impacted patient management through postponement of elective surgeries.[1-5] Cohen et al.[6] suggested that during the pandemic each patient should be evaluated individually with added measures for the protection of both the patient and healthcare professionals. Therefore, we conducted a study evaluating patients' and hospital workers' health status during the pandemic in Turkey.
