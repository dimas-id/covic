At the time of writing, a total of 31,855 people have died from Covid-19 infection in the UK. (Department of Health and Social Care 2020). This incudes at least 90 health and social care workers. Every Thursday evening people across the UK stand outside their houses and clap, bang pots and sing the praise of the National Health Service (NHS) and Social Care staff for putting their lives on the line to fight Covid-19.
