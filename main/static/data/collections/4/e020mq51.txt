We have recently reported for the first time that SARS‐CoV‐2 maybe a bat‐originated coronavirus with a recombination occurred within the spike (S) protein gene based on phylogenetic and simplot analyses(1). These two conclusions are supported by findings recently reported by others and are well accepted in the field of SARS‐CoV‐2 research(2‐4). This article is protected by copyright. All rights reserved.
