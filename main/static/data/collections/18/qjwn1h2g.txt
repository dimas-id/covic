Between January 24 and March 10, of a total of 2,370 individuals who had contacted the first 30 cases of COVID19, 13 were found to have COVID19, resulting secondary attack rate of 0.55% (95% CI 0.31 to 0.96). Of 119 household contacts, 9 had infections resulting secondary attack rate of 7.56 (95% CI 3.73 to 14.26).
