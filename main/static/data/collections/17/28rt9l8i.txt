The outbreak of the novel corona virus had dramatic impact throughout the World. It was completely unexpected for most of us and we were not prepared at all to change our daily routines. When the first cases of deaths due to the novel corona virus were announced, the first reaction was an acute interest followed by some degree of ignorance and many people thought that the situation was being exaggerated. In a very short time, the novel corona virus became extremely popular in the media and sat right at the centre of attraction.
