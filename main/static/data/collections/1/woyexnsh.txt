Abstract This article describes the rapid mitigation strategies in addressing the rising numbers of coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) cases in Singapore. Learning from the severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) experience in 2003, early preparation started in January 2020 when Wuhan was declared as the epicentre of the epidemic. The government had constructed a three-pronged approach which includes travel, healthcare, and community measures to curb the spread of COVID-19.
