●. In the United States, morbidity and mortality from COVID-19 infection varies substantially among populations and geographic regions; ●. Variation has been attributed to chronic disease burden, and certain sociodemographic factors; ●. We developed an index of COVID-19 vulnerability using a regression modeling approach with observed diagnosis at the Zipcode level in Washington State; ●. The resulting index can be used by policy makers for the targeting of public health and health care resources.
