Covid-19 is a viral disease, also known as SARS-Cov-2, was first reported in Wuhan China, December 2019. Respiratory manifestations from the induced acute lung injury were the most common reported findings. Few cases showed extrapulmonary manifestations. Covid-19 associated neurological manifestations have not been widely reported. In this report, we describe a case of encephalopathy in a patient with Covid-19 infection.
