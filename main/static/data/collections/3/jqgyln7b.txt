• Awake, Emergency, Intervention for an Unsecured 'Unable to intubate' Critical upper airway obstruction is a surgical challenge. • Aerosolization and risk COVID 19 transmission add to the already existing difficulty of surgical restoration of critical airway obstruction. • Adequate personal protection, rapid restoration of the airway with the minimum aerosol generation, and prevention of COVID 19 dissemination are keys to the success of Emergency Tracheostomy during the Pandemic.
