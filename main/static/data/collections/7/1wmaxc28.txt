COVID19 has forced a sea change in the practice of dermatology across the world in 2020. Some changes enforced upon us will be long standing after the horrific pandemic and current deployment of many dermatologists to internal medicine. Whilst attention is currently focussed upon managing the pandemic and its immediate impact of COVID19 upon dermatology departments,1 it is instructive to consider the ways in which dermatology (and medicine more broadly) will change in the POST CORONA era.
