Data sources Information about health services provision of public tertiary dental hospitals in China mainland was retrieved through a web search engine. Data analysis The status of non-emergency dental services, emergency dental services and online professional consultation was recorded and analysed. If telehealth was provided, the charge,means of access, service time and service content were investigated. The geographical distribution of hospitals was recorded and classified .Results A total of 48 dental hospitals were included in the study. All 48 dental hospitals suspended general non-emergency dental treatment. They provided emergency dental services only.
