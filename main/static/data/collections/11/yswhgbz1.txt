Novel coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) was declared a global pandemic on March, 2020.1 Recalcati et al. reported cutaneous manifestations in 20.4% of COVID-19 patients.2 We identified six patients (five female, one male) with COVID-19-associated cutaneous manifestations in April 2020 on the inpatient dermatology consultation service. The mean age was 51 years (range, 28-71 years).
