The British Dental Association (BDA) has applauded the call from 101 MPs - from 7 parties - who have joined forces to demand immediate action from the Chancellor to save the UK's dental services Fleur Anderson, Labour MP for Putney, Roehampton and Southfields has led efforts in parliament, delivering a stark warning to the government that unless urgent action is taken dental practices across the country will not recover from the COVID-19 crisis
