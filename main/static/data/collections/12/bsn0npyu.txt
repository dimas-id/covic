Stress refers to situations in which one feels that the demands imposed on them exceed the limitations of their ability to adapt. This causes a series of adverse consequences among which is compromised health (Lazarus and Folkman, 1984). On 11th March 2020, the World Health Organization declared the pandemic status of COVID‐19 diseases, leading to the European Union states imposing the lockdown, Croatia being one among them. Infection prevention became the nation's most important goal, and people had to change their day‐to‐day habits.
