The new coronavirus SARS‐COV‐2 is currently impacting life around the globe (1). The rapid spread of this viral disease might be highly challenging for health care systems. This was seen in Northern Italy and in New York City for example(2). Governments reacted with different measures such as shutdown of all schools, universities and up to a general curfew. All of those measures have a huge impact on the economy.
