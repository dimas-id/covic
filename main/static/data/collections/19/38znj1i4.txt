BACKGROUND: The coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID‐19) has infected millions of people worldwide, particularly in older adults. The first cases of possible re‐infection by SARS‐CoV‐2 were reported in April 2020 among older adults. DESIGN/SETTING: In this brief report, we present three geriatric cases with two episodes of SARS‐CoV‐2 infection separated by a symptom‐free interval.
