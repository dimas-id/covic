This Preview summarizes the Ada Lovelace Institute rapid evidence review Exit through the App Store?, which sets out proposals for whether, and how, the UK government should use technology to transition from the COVID-19 global public health crisis. It examines the potential development and implementation of technical solutions to support symptom tracking, contact tracing, and immunity certification. The full rapid evidence review takes into account societal, political, legal, and ethical perspectives and gives findings and recommendations for the transition and rebuild phases that follow containment, delay, and mitigation.
