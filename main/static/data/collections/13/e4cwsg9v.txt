During the Covid-19 pandemic, dengue cases have been increased in most of the dengue-endemic countries in the world. Therefore, in these countries, both Covid-19 and Dengue are occurring simultaneously. The dengue-endemic countries are at the risk of possible co-infection and co-epidemics in where Covid-19 and dengue disease are co-existing, and the viruses are cocirculating.
