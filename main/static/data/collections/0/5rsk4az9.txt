Time dependent spread of Covid-19 among the population of the UK, the USA and India is analyzed using a recently developed mathematical model [1-3]. Results of model predictions of case growth in these countries during the next six weeks are also presented. The model is applicable to case studies and near term predictions for other countries and regions. Key words: Coronavirus, Covid-19, SARS, model, analysis, data, hospital, ICU, infectious disease, UK, USA, India
