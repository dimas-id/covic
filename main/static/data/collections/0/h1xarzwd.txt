A novel coronavirus (severe acute respiratory syndrome corononavirus‐2; SARS‐CoV‐2) has affected millions of people across the world. The coronavirus disease (COVID‐19) resulting from SARS‐CoV‐2 manifests in variable clinical severity, featuring both respiratory and extra‐respiratory symptoms. Dermatological manifestations of COVID‐19 are sparsely reported. To study the various dermatological findings in SARS‐CoV‐2 positive patients in Indian population.
