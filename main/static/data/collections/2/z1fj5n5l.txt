Abstract The outbreak of 2019 novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) has been a major epidemic threat all over the world. Such a public health emergency can greatly impact various aspects of people's health and lives. This paper focused on its potential risks on reproductive health, including reproductive system and function, as well as gamete and embryo development, which was underpinned by the virus, drugs, chemical disinfectants and panic psychology during the outbreak of COVID-19.
