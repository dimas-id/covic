When the Chief Dental Officer for England announced that Urgent Dental Centres (UDCs) were to open during the COVID-19 pandemic, there were numerous hurdles that needed to be overcome prior to opening One of the biggest challenges, in addition to securing a supply of appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE), was the provision of training and testing of respiratory protective equipment (RPE) and the appropriate use of PPE in order to ensure that the dental workforce is safe to deliver face to face patient care
