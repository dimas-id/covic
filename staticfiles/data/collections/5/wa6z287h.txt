Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is caused by Severe acute respiratory syndrome CoV-2 (SARS-CoV-2) and represents the causative agent of a potentially fatal disease that is declared a pandemic by the World Health Organization (WHO) on March 11, 2020. There remain a considerable number of questions that need to be answered to be able to understand this virus. Herein, we evaluated whether there is a relationship between covid-19 and complement 3 and complement 4 levels.
