In December 2019 a novel coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) emerged in Wuhan, China, responsible for an aggressive interstitial pneumonia. Italy was the first western country to be hit by the CoronaVIrus Disease 2019 (COVID-19) and on 9 March our Prime Minister announced a nationwide lockdown, strictly forbidding any contacts outside cohabitants, except for urgent or medical reasons. In compliance with the ministerial decree, all scheduled visits were suspended, maintaining hospital access only for emergencies.
