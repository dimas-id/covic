A 21-year-old woman (primipara) at 33w+6 d gestation was admitted to the Third Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical University on February 3, 2020. None of her prenatal screening results were abnormal. She complained of cold symptoms on January 29, 2020. Upon onset a fever of 39°C on January 31, she went to a local clinic, where her blood pressure was 170/110 mmHg. Her white blood cell count was 10.92×109 /L, total lymphocyte count was 0.85×10 9 /L, hemoglobin was 9.8 g/dl, and platelet count was 56×1010 /L and acetaminophen was given.
