As the SARS‐CoV2 pandemic is still progressing, dissemination of information has become of paramount importance. However, the rapid influx of SARS‐CoV‐2 patients in our hospitals has overwhelmed the time needed for appropriate information interpretation and its application. Noteworthy, awareness among clinicians is growing about the systemic involvement triggered by SARS‐CoV‐2 infection. Here we focused on two emerging clinical feature associated with SARS‐CoV‐2 infection with potential long‐term relevance.
