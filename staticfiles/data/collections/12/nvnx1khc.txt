• Confinement and prolonged periods of inactivity incure various health risks and increase levels of stress, depression, and anxiety. • Being physically active is a simple and effective way of addressing these negative effects. • Even in confinement and limited free movement people can remain physically active. • Prevention of COVID-19 infection should not increase other health risks. • Exercise professionals should encourage organizations, local authorities, and governments to promote PA during this period of mass quarantine.
