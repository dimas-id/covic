• Left cardiac sympathetic denervation is reported to reduce the rate of events and sudden death in young patients with channelopathy. • Horner syndrome and recurrence can be seen after left cardiac sympatetic denervation. • Bilateral cardiac sympathetic denervation in pediatric patient, is reported as a case report. • As a result of our clinical experience bilateral cardiac sympathetic denervation is effective and safe procedure which reduces recurrence in pediatric patients with channelopathies.
