A very interesting report, for the first time, provided direct evidence that the ongoing pandemic etiological agent, SARS‐CoV‐2, may be able to directly infect central nervous system (CNS) through an endothelial route. If proven true, this may explain frequent neurological symptoms associated with severe COVID‐19 diseases. A corresponding therapeutic intervention will be urgently needed to prevent such dangerous infections of CNS and associated high mortality rate. This article is protected by copyright.
