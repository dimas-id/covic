• The Korean Centers for Disease Control & Prevention reports the asymptomatic case proportion for COVID-19 is 33.3%. • The asymptomatic case proportion in South Korea closely approximates the rate of 30.8% reported by Nishiura et al. (2020) among Japanese nationals returning from abroad. • The asymptomatic case proportion in South Korea is higher (56.1%) among those who re-test positive for COVID-19.
