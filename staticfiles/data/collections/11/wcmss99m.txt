COVID - 19 has been one of the greatest disasters in human history. It has completely disrupted our lives. With regard to medical practice, it changed the way doctors started treating patients. One of the positives of COVID - 19 has been the emergnece of telemedicine in treating chronic conditions like diabetes. In India, telemedicine which was illegal was rapidly legalised and gudelines for telemedicine were drawn up. This commentay describes our approach to treating diabetes patiens during the COVID lockdown period based on experience at a large tertiary diabetes centre in India.
