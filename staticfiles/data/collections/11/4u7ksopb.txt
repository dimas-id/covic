This chapter focuses on what happens once you have safely arrived at the destination; security risks relating to online acquisition of currency, in-country transportation, etc. will be discussed. Security for you, your technology, and your data is next as we discuss how to protect those through various personal health and medical management apps, Wi-Fi security awareness in foreign states and hotels, Tripit, online censorship laws, etc.
