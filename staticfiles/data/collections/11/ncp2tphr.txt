In early December 2019, the acute respiratory illness began in the Wuhan, China, which quickly spread around the world, that today known as COVID-19. (Sun, Qie, Liu, Ren, & Xi, 2020) Preliminary studies have shown that hospitalized patients have different symptoms, including myalgia, whooping cough, fatigue, and dyspnea and gastrointestinal complains. (Guo et al., 2020; Zhang et al., 2020) In more recent studies, skin manifestations have also been reported in covid-19 patients.
