Members of the National Association of Addiction Treatment Providers (NAATP) ? treatment programs both inpatient and outpatient ? are still open for business In fact, business is booming, NAATP told ADAW last week This is good for patients, and good for providers, but at the same time, it's stressful for staff Employers are working to combat this stress by helping to obtain personal protective equipment (PPE) ? masks and gloves ? for staff In addition, they are trying to ramp up with telehealth, provide supports for staff and keep paying attention to the needs of patients Demand for treatment is increasing, as drinking is going up as well
