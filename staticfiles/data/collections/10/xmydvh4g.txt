While Severe-Acute-Respiratory-Syndrome-CoronaVirus-2 (SARSCoV-2/Covid-19) causes physical morbidity for healthcare workers (HCWs), Covid-19 also carries psychological morbidity for HCWs. This morbidity translates to anxiety and depressive symptomatology. To this end, we assessed anxiety and depression among HCWs during Covid-19 pandemic.
