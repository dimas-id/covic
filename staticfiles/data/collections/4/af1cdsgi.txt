Abstract Recent outbreak of Coronavirus (COVID-19) has impacted the health services globally. The UK has experienced the second highest deaths in the world (after USA) of over 40,000 people due to COVID-19. In the beginning of March 2020, as a precaution most NHS hospital trust halted the organ transplant services due to outbreak of COVID-19 and completed ceased the live-donors facility. This has severely affected the waiting time especially for patients from ethnic minorities.
