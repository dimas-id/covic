In a recent short communication “Oral vesiculobullous lesions associated with SARS‐CoV‐2 infection” with a running title “Oral manifestations in COVID‐19 patients”, Martín Carreras‐Presaset. al (2020) described three patients who developed oral ulcerative lesions during the COVID‐19 lock‐down period; a 56‐year‐old healthy male, a 58‐year‐old‐male with diabetes and hypertension and a 65‐year‐old female with hypertension.
