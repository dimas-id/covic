We read with great interest the recent article "Analysis of 92 deceased patients with COVID-19" by Yang et al. published in the Journal of medical virology. The authors observed that 14 patients suffered renal injury after infection of SARS-CoV-2 in 92 deceased patients with coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19), although none of them had chronic renal insufficiency.
