A novel human coronavirus(SARS‐CoV‐2) causing a severe acute respiratory syndrome, coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID‐19), was reported in Wuhan, China at the end of 2019(Wang, Horby, Hayden, & Gao,2020; Khan, Ali, Siddique, Nabi, 2020).The infectionhas an estimated incubation period of 1‐14 days and its clinical manifestations and symptoms include cough, fever and shortness of breath (Zhuet al., 2020).
