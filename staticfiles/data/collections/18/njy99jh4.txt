Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID‐19) is mainly an acute respiratory infectious disease caused by a novel coronavirus (officially named Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2, SARS‐CoV‐2) in December 2019, which is currently a worldwide pandemic, mainly causes the novel coronavirus pneumonia (NCP). At present, it mainly relies on Real‐time RT‐PCR to detect SARS‐CoV‐2 virus nucleic acid collected from the clinical specimens of patients as the standard for diagnosis, discontinuation of quarantine and discharge.(1,2) This article is protected by copyright.
